\[

\[

-tr\log\!\left(-\frac{1}{4}\frac{\partial^2}{\partial\tau^2}\right)

And
\[

\[

So 
\[
\log{\frac{\Det\!\left(-\frac{1}{4}\frac{\partial^2}{\partial\tau^2}+\frac{B_{ab}}{4\bar{T}}\right)}{\Det\!\left(-\frac{1}{4}\frac{\partial^2}{\partial\tau^2}\right)}}
